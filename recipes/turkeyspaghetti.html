Turkey Spaghetti with Kielbasa Meatballs

This is Turkey Spaghetti. It is one of my favorite dishes. Using a non-traditional meat to liven up a traditional dish, this recipe aims to excite the tastebuds in a marvelous manner. This dish also uses a unique addition to simulate meatballs. Using panfried kielbasa is the perfect touch to spice this dish up.

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Turkey
  • Half of a Kielbasa
  • Half of an onion
  • 1 whole bell pepper
  • 1 whole tomato
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic minced
  • 1/2 pack whole wheat noodles
